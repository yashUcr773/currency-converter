Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2025

Information We Collect

Currency Converter Pro is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Storage: Your currency preferences, pinned currencies, and custom exchange rates are stored locally in your browser
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies

How We Use Information

All data is stored locally on your device to:

  • Remember your pinned currencies between sessions
  • Store custom exchange rates for "what-if" scenarios
  • Cache exchange rates for offline functionality

Third-Party Services

We use ExchangeRate-API to fetch current exchange rates. Please review their privacy policy at: https://exchangerate-api.com/terms

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data. No data is transmitted to our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
